Ticket Game Simulator

Strategic Level Progression Game

How to Play

Navigate through 50 levels using tickets as currency. Your goal is to reach level 50 without running out of tickets.

Game Mechanics:

  • Each level has a 75% chance of success and 25% chance of failure (except safe levels)
  • Safe levels occur every 5 levels (5, 10, 15, etc.) with guaranteed success
  • Failing a level doesn't reset progress, but continuing costs additional tickets
  • Failure costs increase: 1st failure free, then 1, 2, 4, 8, 10+ tickets
  • Express tickets allow starting at level 20, reducing total risk

Success Rate Information:

• 316 tickets are needed for 100% success rate (reaching level 50)

• 206 tickets are needed for 100% success rate with an express ticket start
